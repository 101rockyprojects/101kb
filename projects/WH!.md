# Projects

This folder documents my projects: what I built, why I chose certain approaches, and what I learned.

## What belongs here

- Project overviews: goals, scope, constraints, target users.
- Architecture decisions and tradeoffs.
- Postmortems: what worked, what didn't, what I'd do next time.

## Conventions

- Keep one "hub" note per project (overview + links to sub-notes).
- When you mention a concept, link it: `[[concepts/...]]`.
- When you mention a tool, link it: `[[tools/...]]`.

## Suggested Links

- Concepts index: [[concepts/WH!]]
- Tools index: [[tools/WH!]]
