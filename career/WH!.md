# Career

This folder collects non-code learnings that still affect engineering outcomes: project management, communication, decision-making, and notes from coursework.

## What belongs here

- Frameworks and methods (e.g. PESTEL, DMAIC, Design Thinking).
- University notes and summaries.
- Soft skills: collaboration patterns, leadership, feedback, planning.

## Conventions

- Prefer summaries over raw links: keep the key takeaways in the note.
- When a note is mostly a resource list, add a short "What I learned" section.
- Link to projects where the idea was applied:
  - Example: a note about governance can link to [[projects/WH!]] entries.
