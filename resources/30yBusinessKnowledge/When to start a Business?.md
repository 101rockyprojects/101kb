# Now the *Idea*
If you love writing, start to find a partner that helps you, a mentor, a publisher, an agent...
It's your passion what fuels the success.

## 1. Execution
What is the first you can do to make your business idea come true?
- A blog?
- A linkedin post?
- A social media?
> DO NOT MAKE IT HARD TO YOURSELF!

The execution maybe not *brilliant* or *the best* but it's the first step, doesn't must to be PERFECT.
Even if you give it for free at the start to get clients and visibility.
## 2. Where the revenue is going to come from?
Revenue is not very clear at the beginning. DO NOT limit yourself.
Be brave, EXPERIMENT!

What's the best combination for you?
- Per hour
- Per day
- Per output performance

## 3. The purpose MUST to be STRONG
Yes, success and profit moves, and enjoy keep you motivated the first week, but...
HOW are you going to make a difference in the world?
HOW are you going to make sure that people want to work with you?
HOW are you going to make sure that what you are doing is not just for profit?

If you have a SOLID purpose BIGGER than yourself, identify and install it in your business, you are going to grow, to keep people calling you. You don't have to manage people, but your purpose.

### EXAMPLE
#### Airbnb
They started a company basically by renting out space in their own apartment. They call them crazy, BUT IT WORKS! They know that, when conventions and big events came, the demand of spaces near them rises, and they will take advantage of that.
