The success of a business comes from a few factors:
# How to win
## 1. Delay Gratification
You don't rush to charge customers, you let them help you succeed.
Maybe the first customer is a free work. Maybe the second... But one client happy is 3 potential customers calling you in the future.

## 2. Culture
The company MUST have client-centric culture.
**Amazon** bring value, the focus on their clients, follow them, promote them, share them on their stories, show them love.
Most people DON'T DO THIS.

A customer is not only a profit is something you cared about.

## 3. Luck
Luck can be hackable:
	1. **Being persistent**: Keep going, the competitors can be less or more, but if you keep moving most of them will stay back. More time in the game = more opportunities.
	2. **Knowing your destination**: What do you want? What make you happy? Aim for big company.
	3. **Taking risks**: Love risks, embrace fear. Working hard is not equal to success, nurses are not billionaires. The most risk you take, the luckier you get.

> In long term you will win. Accept that you could lose everything, and it does not matter.
> Do not rush, is not a sprint. It is a mountain, enjoy the journey, firm steps.

Monetize can wait. Build something you users use and need. BE CAREFUL, focus on the user, not the advertizer. 

# How to lose
Yes, you have to allow you fail, to be successful. Accept losing, is key.
## 1. Don't let things on YOU
You will be controlled by the very thing that gives you pleasure. Not the correct way.

## 2. Turn your EGO off
External ego, ego of being seen. Enjoy being underestimated, by your competition, even by your customers. If people think you can't, and you do, then brilliant.

## 3. Take your time...
Do not let someone else to decide your success. Internal ego, ego of know what are you.
What you do on your day, what you want, what you love is by your own.
