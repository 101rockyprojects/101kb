# Resources

This folder is a curated library: books, papers, articles, courses, and videos that influenced how I build software.

## What belongs here

- Links with context: why it's worth consuming, and what to extract from it.
- Notes taken while reading/watching.
- Short summaries that point to deeper notes in `concepts/`, `tools/`, or `career/`.

## Conventions

- Prefer "resource + takeaways" over link dumps.
- When a resource teaches a concept, link the concept note:
  - Example: a testing book -> [[concepts/Pirámide de Testing]]

## Suggested Links

- Concepts index: [[concepts/WH!]]
- Career index: [[career/WH!]]
